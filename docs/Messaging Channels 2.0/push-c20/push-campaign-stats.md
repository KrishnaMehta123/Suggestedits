# View Push Campaign Stats

Once the campaign is live, you can view its stats by navigating to *Push* under *Campaigns* on the CleverTap dashboard and selecting the campaign of interest to view its deliveries, clicks, and conversions.

<Image title="push_filter_campaigns.png" alt={1105} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/9d5c791-push_filter_campaigns.png" />

Click to open the selected campaign. 

![6026](https://files.readme.io/c9f27cc-push_stats_hero.png "push_stats_hero.png")

Under the *Sent Conversions* card, you can track the following stats for push notification campaigns:

* Qualified: The number of users qualified for the campaign.  
* Sent: The total number of notifications delivered to user devices.\
   Impressions: The total number of devices that receive the notifications. Calculated with 
  * (Impressions/Sent) *100. Available only for CleverTap SDK 3.5.1 and above.\&#xA;* Uplift: Click to get details of uplift achieved by Push Amplification and sending to App inbox.\
    Clicks: Number of clicks generated by the campaign. Calculated as (Clicks/Sent) \* 100.
* CTR: The clickthrough rate of the campaign. It is measured by (Click/Impressions) \* 100.\
  Converted users: The number of users that converted after receiving the campaign. 
* Errors: Number of errors for the campaign. These errors can be both technical errors such as FCM related errors or non-technical errors such as messages frequency exceeded.
* Control group: The number of users considered in the control group for the campaign.
* View through conversions: The users who converted after viewing the notification. View through conversions are measured by *Users who converted after viewing / Users who were sent the notification* \* 100.
* Click through conversions: The users who converted after clicking the notification. Click through conversions are measured by *Users who converted after clicking / Users who were sent the notification* 100\*.

<Image title="2 Sent Conversions.png" alt={1571} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/42fac73-2_Sent_Conversions.png" />

* Message Trend: Shows the daily, weekly, and monthly trends for campaign messages. 

<Image title="push_stats_message_trend.png" alt={1346} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/5e571bc-push_stats_message_trend.png" />

Conversion Performance: Shows the Conversion performance, Revenue performance, Users conversion funnel, and Influenced conversion funnel. 

<Image title="push_stats_users_conversion_funnel.png" alt={1325} border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/5c2ba7e-push_stats_users_conversion_funnel.png">
  Users Conversion Funnel
