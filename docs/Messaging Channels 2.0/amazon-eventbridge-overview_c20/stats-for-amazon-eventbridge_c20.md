After the campaign is live, you can view the campaign stats from the dashboard by navigating to the *Campaigns* page and selecting the campaign of interest to view the following stats: Events Streamed, Errors, and Control Group.

<Image title="Eventbridge Stats.png" alt={2692} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/aed1ceb-Eventbridge_Stats.png" />

* **Events Streamed**: This indicates the count of events that were exported to Eventbridge.
* **Errors**: This indicates the counts of events that were not exported to Eventbridge due to some error. The details of errors are described under the Errors section.
* **Control group**: The number of times the user qualifies for the control group.
