Once the campaign has been published, you can view the statistics from the dashboard. Click **Campaigns** > select the specific SMS campaign from the campaign list. A Stats page opens up where you can view the overall conversion performance and errors.

<Image title="s1.png" alt={2880} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/0f63ea3-s1.png" />

* **Sent**: Represents the total number of messages sent to the users.
* **Converted users**: Represents the percentage of users who perform the conversion event in the campaign.

<Image title="s2.png" alt={2880} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/759faa6-s2.png" />

The **Errors** tab represents the errors that occurred while sending the SMS to the user.
