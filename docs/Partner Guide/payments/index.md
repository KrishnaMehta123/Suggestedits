CleverTap partners with In-App Purchasing platforms such as [RevenueCat](doc:revenuecat) to offer a solution that directly sends events related to customer purchases from your app into CleverTap. With this integration, you can analyze events and monitor revenue from your app seamlessly on the CleverTap dashboard.
