Leveraging TikTok's audience can significantly enhance your remarketing efforts. With its highly engaged and diverse user base, TikTok provides powerful targeting capabilities to help marketers retarget users who have previously interacted with their content or shown interest in related products. By utilizing TikTok's advanced targeting features, you can ensure that your remarketing campaigns reach the right audience at the right time.
