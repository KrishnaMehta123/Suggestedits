CleverTap integrates with messaging partners like [Taxi for Email](doc:taxi-for-email) to: 

* Design, code, and manage email templates using a messaging template partner (currently, Taxi for Email) 
* Segment audiences, personalize content, and execute campaigns on the CleverTap dashboard. 

This helps effectively target the right users with tailored messages.
