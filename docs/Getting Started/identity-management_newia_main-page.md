Identity management is how CleverTap attributes properties and events performed on your application to a specific user. It is the process of defining your users' information on the CleverTap dashboard by providing each user with a unique identifier.

This section covers the following topics in detail:

* [Identity Management Overview](https://docs.clevertap.com/v4.0/docs/identity-management_introduction)
* [Handling Identities](https://docs.clevertap.com/v4.0/docs/handling-identities)
* [Assigning Identities](https://dash.readme.com/project/clevertap-user-docs/v4.0/docs/assigning-identities)
* [Identifying Users](https://docs.clevertap.com/v4.0/docs/identifying-users)
* [Identification Methods](https://docs.clevertap.com/v4.0/docs/identification-methods)
* [Advanced Identity Management](https://docs.clevertap.com/v4.0/docs/advanced-identity-management)
* [Best Practices - Identity Management](https://docs.clevertap.com/v4.0/docs/best-practices-identity-management)
* [Troubleshooting Identity Management](https://docs.clevertap.com/v4.0/docs/troubleshooting-identity-management)
