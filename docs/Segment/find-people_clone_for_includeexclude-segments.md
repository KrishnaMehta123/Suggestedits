##Include/Exclude Segments

You can simplify complex queries by including or excluding user segments. You can create powerful segmentation that is valid for a variety of scenarios. 

For example, you may want to offer discounts to all the users who have expressed interest by adding the product to cart in the past 30 days. However, you want to save your engagement dollars by excluding users who are already familiar with the product and use it regularly. 
The parameters for these users can be the following:
  * Users who have charged 3 times in the past 3 months, 
  * Users who have launched the app 15 times in the past 1 month
  * Users who rated a product 10 times in the past year
