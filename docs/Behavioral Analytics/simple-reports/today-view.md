# Overview

The *Today* dashboard gives you a snapshot and trend analysis of your active and engaged users and their activities in your application across your apps and website.

<Image title="Today_board_main.png" alt={996} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/efea8e0-Today_board_main.png" />

To get to this view, navigate to *Dashboard* > *Today*.

This view shows:

* *Active Users* represent users that have launched the application on the selected date. 

<Image title="Today_board_active_users.png" alt={765} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/b154780-Today_board_active_users.png" />

* *New Users* represent first-time users in your app or website on that day.
* *Returning Users* represent users returning to your app or website on that day.

<Image title="Today_board_new_vs_active_users.png" alt={775} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/37500f2-Today_board_new_vs_active_users.png" />

* *Usage Source* shows the split of total users on a select day by platform.

<Image title="Today_board_usage_source.png" alt={585} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/2992656-Today_board_usage_source.png" />

In addition, the *Activity Stream* dashboard shows some of the users who are active in your app and the events that they are undertaking. Navigate to *Boards* > *Today* > *Activity Stream* tab.
