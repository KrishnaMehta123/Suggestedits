# Overview

The Net Promoter Score (*NPS*) dashboard provides you with a detailed view of the overall NPS feedback received from your customers. To view the NPS dashboard, navigate to *Dashboard* > *Boards* > *NPS*.  

# NPS Board

The NPS board represents the following data points:

* Overall *NPS score*
* *Total responses received* from the customers

You can filter the data for distinct campaigns using the campaign filter. You can also filter data for a specified time period using the date filter.

Refer to the image below for a better understanding.

<Image title="NPS BOARD.png" alt={2274} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/e310576-NPS_BOARD.png" />

The NPS board also provides:

* Bar charts representing data for respective NPS buckets of Detractors, Passives, and Promoters.
* NPS Score Trends over a specific period of time (for example daily, weekly, monthly).
* NPS trends based on buckets for Detractors, Passives, and Promoters over a specific period of time (for example daily, weekly, monthly).  
* A bar graph view of the *Ratings Distribution* over the past 30 days.

<Image title="nps2.1.png" alt={1528} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/78446b8-nps2.1.png" />

Additionally, you can also view the trend chart for *Total Responses Received* on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis.
