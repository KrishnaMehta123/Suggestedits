# Overview

If your app charges customers for goods or services, you can use CleverTap to track revenue. You can record the *Charged* event to track customer purchases and view revenue-related metrics.

<Image title="d2d91ec-Analytics_View_Revenue.png" alt={1698} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/4dbd228-d2d91ec-Analytics_View_Revenue.png" />

To get to this view, navigate to *Dashboard* > *Revenue*.

To display revenue data, set your *Charged* event as your *Conversion Event* and specify the revenue event property (e.g. amount) to be calculated and displayed.
