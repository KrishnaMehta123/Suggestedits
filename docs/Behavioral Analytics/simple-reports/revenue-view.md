# Overview

If your app charges customers for goods or services, you can use CleverTap to track revenue. You can record the *Charged* event to track customer purchases, and view revenue related metrics.

To get to this view, navigate to *Dashboard* > *Revenue*.

To display revenue data, please be sure to set your *Charged* event as your *Conversion Event* and specify the revenue event property (i.e. *Amount*) to be calculated and displayed.
