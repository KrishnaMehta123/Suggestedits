Understanding user behavior is vital for growth marketers and product teams looking to drive growth and increase engagement. By leveraging Behavioral Analytics, you can gain insights into how users interact with your product and identify areas for improvement.

Behavioral Analytics provides you with insights into the performance of your product, including conversion, retention, and engagement. This allows you to identify which features or aspects of your product are driving growth and which ones need improvement.

By analyzing user behavior with Behavioral Analytics, you can optimize your product to increase user retention, improve conversion rates, and ultimately drive growth. So if you are a growth marketer or product team, you can leverage Analytics to gain a deeper understanding of your users and achieve your growth goals.

Behavioral Analytics offers a variety of powerful tools for tracking and analyzing user behavior. These are:

## Boards

Boards are a way to save and consume analysis in a recurring manner. It provides a high-level view of the key metrics related to a campaign or workflow, such as the number of users who received a message, the number of users who clicked on a link, and the conversion rate. For more information, refer to [Boards](doc:simple-reports).

## Events

The event analysis lets you monitor specific user actions or events, such as clicks, views, and purchases. For more information, refer to [Analyze Events](doc:events-analytics).

## Funnels

Funnels enable you to track user behavior across different conversion funnel stages, such as the number of users who visited your website, added items to their cart, and completed a purchase. For more information, refer to [Funnels](doc:funnels).

## Cohorts

Cohort analysis is a module that enables you to track retention and analyze user behavior over time by grouping users into cohorts based on shared characteristics, such as their sign-up date or behavior. For more information, refer to [Cohorts](doc:cohorts).

## Trends

Trends enable businesses to track and analyze changes in user behavior over time providing visual representations of trends in user engagement. For more information, refer to [Trends](doc:trends).

## Pivots

Pivots is a CleverTap feature that supports complex analysis involving multiple dimensions and complex data. Pivots enable businesses to analyze user behavior across different dimensions, such as location, device, or behavior. For more information, refer to [Pivots](doc:pivots).

## Flows

Flows enable businesses to track and analyze the user journey across different channels, such as mobile apps, websites, and email campaigns. For more information, refer to [Flows](doc:flows).

## Real Impact

Real Impact enables businesses to measure the impact of their marketing campaigns on user behavior and revenue. For more information, refer to [Real Impact](doc:real-impact).

## Session Analytics

Session analytics helps businesses to analyze user behavior during a session or visit to their app or website. For more information, refer to [Session Analytics](doc:session-analytics).
