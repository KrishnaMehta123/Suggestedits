# Overview

If your app charges customers for goods or services – you an use CleverTap to track revenue. You can record the Charged Event to track customer purchases, and view revenue related metrics via the Dashboard → Revenue.

<Image title="board-4.png" alt={1399} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/284c2c5-board-4.png" />

To display revenue data, please be sure to set your Charged event as your “Conversion Event” and specify the revenue event property (i.e. Amount) to be calculated and displayed.
