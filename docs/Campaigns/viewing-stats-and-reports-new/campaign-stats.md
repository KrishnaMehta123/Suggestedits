Campaign Stats\
Hero numbers\
When you click on a campaign, you are taken to the stats page. All the key metrics are showcased at the top of the stats page.

Funnels

The stats page also shows the sent and viewed funnels. This helps you better analyse the impact of the campaign.

Conversion stats

The conversions section on the stats page will help you understand the impact at one glance since it also showcases control group conversion stats.
