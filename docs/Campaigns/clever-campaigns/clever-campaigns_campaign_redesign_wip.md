CleverTap campaigns are pre-built marketing campaigns to provide the capability to engage your users. These campaigns can also retain acquired users, drive app engagement, and help in user win-backs.

#Get Started with a Clever Campaign
To create a clever campaign, perform the following steps:
1. From the dashboard, navigate to the *Campaigns* tab.
2. Click the **Create Clever campaigns** link.
