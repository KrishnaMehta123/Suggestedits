# Overview

This document presents a comprehensive collection of predefined messaging templates offered by CleverTap. The templates provided are designed to enhance customer engagement across multiple channels. You can leverage these templates to save time and effort while delivering compelling and personalized messages to your users. CleverTap offers a comprehensive range of messaging channels that cater to diverse user preferences and engagement goals.
