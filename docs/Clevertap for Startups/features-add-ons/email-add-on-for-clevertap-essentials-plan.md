# Overview

The Email Add-on for CleverTap Essentials Plan enables you to streamline email communication without the need to integrate an external Email Service Provider (ESP). This add-on leverages a seamless integration with SendGrid, allowing businesses to send emails directly from CleverTap.

With features such as AMP for Email, Email on Acid API support, and robust consumption tracking, the add-on provides startups with advanced tools to optimize their email campaigns and enhance customer engagement.

# Features of the Email Add-on

When you purchase the CleverTap Email Add-on, you gain access to the following features:

- **Monthly Email Quota**: 1,00,000 emails per month.
- **SendGrid Integration**: A dedicated SendGrid account, including one IP.
- **AMP for Email**: Create engaging, interactive email content.
- **Email on Acid (EoA)**: Test your email design and deliverability with up to 5 API calls per month.
- **Rich Content Support**: Leverage linked content and Liquid Tags for dynamic personalization.

## Purchase the Email Add-on

1. Log in to your CleverTap dashboard.
2. Navigate to _Organization >  Billing > My Plan > Manage Add-ons_.
3. Locate the _CleverTap Email Add-on_ and click **Purchase**.
4. Choose a tier based on your anticipated email volume.
5. Confirm the purchase.

## Configure Email Settings

After purchasing the add-on:  

1. Navigate to _Settings > Email > Provider_.  
2. Select _CleverTap Email_ from the dropdown menu.  
3. Provide the necessary credentials as instructed. For the first version, you will receive the SendGrid API key from CleverTap's onboarding team.

## IP Warm-up

To ensure optimal deliverability, follow the **IP Warm-up** guide sent via email post-purchase. Gradual increases in email volume help prevent IP reputation issues.  

### Monitoring Usage

You can track your email consumption via:  

1. **Dashboard Metrics**: Navigate to _Organization > Billing > My Plan > Details_.  
   - Email consumption data is updated every 24 hours.  
2. **Alerts**: Receive platform and email notifications at 80%, 90%, 100%, and 120% of your email usage.  

### Overage Management

- At 120% usage, the "CleverTap Email" provider will be automatically paused. You will receive alerts to upgrade your tier to resume email campaigns.  
- Campaign creation remains enabled for other providers during this time.

### Email Campaign Creation

#### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

**1. What happens if I exceed my email quota?**  
You will be charged $0.0005 per email for overages. At 120% consumption, email campaigns via "CleverTap Email" will be paused until you upgrade your add-on tier.  

**2. How long does it take to activate the add-on?**  
The activation process, including SendGrid account setup, is completed within 72 hours of purchase.  

**3. Can I use multiple email providers in CleverTap?**  
Yes, you can configure and use other providers simultaneously.
