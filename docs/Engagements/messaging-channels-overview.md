# Overview

Messaging channels are digital platforms that enable businesses to communicate with their customers through digital mediums such as Emails, SMS, WhatsApp, Push notifications, etc. 

Leveraging the right messaging channel to engage with your customers increases customer retention and conversion rates. 

Messaging channels offer numerous benefits for customer engagement. Listed below are a few:

- **Real-time communication**: Messaging channels allow businesses to communicate with customers in real time, which can help to improve customer satisfaction and increase engagement. Customers can receive immediate responses to their queries, which can help to build trust and strengthen relationships.
- **Convenient and accessible**: Messaging channels can be accessed from various devices such as mobile phones, tablets, and computers, which makes communication convenient and accessible for customers. 
- **Personalization**: Messaging channels allow businesses to personalize their communication with customers by using their names, past behavior, and other relevant data. Personalized communication helps to improve customer engagement and loyalty.
- **Cost-effective**: Messaging channels are a cost-effective way for businesses to communicate with customers. They can be automated and require fewer resources than traditional communication methods such as phone calls and emails.
- **Enhanced customer support**: Messaging channels enable offering enhanced customer support, such as sending automated messages, providing self-service options, and offering 24/7 support. This can help to improve customer satisfaction and reduce the workload for customer support teams.

Overall, messaging channels can help businesses to improve customer engagement, increase satisfaction, and build stronger relationships with their customers.
