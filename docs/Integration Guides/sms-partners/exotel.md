CleverTap supports sending SMS through Exotel. 

Sign up with Exotel and then follow the steps below in the CleverTap Dashboard for a successful integration.

* In the CleverTap Dashboard, navigate to Settings and click on Integrate SMS.
* Select Exotel.
* In the Exotel SID text box, fill in your Exotel SID number from the Exotel Dashboard.
* In the Exotel Token text box, fill in the Exotel Token value from the Exotel Dashboard.
* In the From Number text box, fill in the ExoPhone number.
