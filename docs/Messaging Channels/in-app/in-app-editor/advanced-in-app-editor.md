# Introduction

CleverTap's **Advanced In-App Builder** allows you to effortlessly create visually appealing and engaging mobile in-app messages that seamlessly align with your brand's look and feel. This guide will walk you through its key features and usage, helping you get the most out of this tool.

<br />

## Key Capabilities

The **Advanced In-App Builder** offers remarkable flexibility and ease of use. Here are its key features:

- **Automatic Scaling**: All content automatically adjusts to fit different device sizes, ensuring consistent presentation across platforms.

- **Custom Positioning**: You can position elements freely inside containers, allowing any layout you envision.

- **Stacking Elements**: Stack images or other elements on top of each other for more complex designs.

- **Styling Options**: The interface exposes numerous styling options for every element, providing detailed control over the appearance.

- **Dismiss Button Controls**: Customize the appearance and behavior of the dismiss button, including its style and placement.

- **Animation**: Add dynamic effects such as slide or fade-in animations to enhance user interaction.

## Builder Overview

The Advanced In-App Builder interface is divided into two main sections:

- **Canvas (Left Side)**: The canvas is where you design your in-app message. It is fully interactive, allowing you to drag and drop elements into place, and directly modify their position and size.

- **Controls & Styling Menu (Right Side)**: This menu contains controls and styling options for the elements on the canvas. Any changes made here are immediately reflected on the canvas in real-time.

## FAQs & Troubleshooting

**Q1: How do I add new elements beyond the default ones?**  
A: To add new elements, use the controls on the right side of the builder. You can drag and drop images, text, and buttons directly into the container.

**Q2: Can I preview my in-app message before sending it out?**  
A: As of now, the Preview & Test feature is limited. You can test by sending the message to a single device first.

**Q3: Are there limitations on device sizes or layouts?**  
A: No, the content is automatically scaled to fit any device size, ensuring consistency across different platforms.
