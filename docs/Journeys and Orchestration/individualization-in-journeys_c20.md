# Overview

While creating a complex orchestration for your users, you would want to send the messages that are personalized to the Users to increase actionability on those messages. The following steps guide you on how you can achieve this within different engagement nodes available on Journeys.

To personalize the messages in Journeys from the Engagement blocks:

1. Click *click to edit* section in an engagement block. The *Message* window appears. 
2. Select *What*  >  *Message Type* to personalize your message.

<Image title="Journeys_campaign_personalization.png" alt={780} className="border" width="smart" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/02868b8-Journeys_campaign_personalization.png" />

3. Click *Go To Editor* to edit the message and personalize it.
