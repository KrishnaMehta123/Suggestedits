#Overview
Based on certain parameters, you can filter journeys to view on the Journey listing page.

##Quick Filters
Quick Filters are available on top for the created journeys.

The Quick Filters are:
- Draft
Selecting this filter displays all journeys that are in draft status. 
- Running
Selecting this filter displays all published and active journeys.
- Complete
Selecting this filter displays all finished journeys; either based on time or segment.
- Stopped
Selecting this filter displays all stopped journeys. You cannot restart these journeys.
- Paused
Selecting this filter displays all paused journeys. You may resume these journeys.

##Custom Filters
You may customize journey filters. To do so.
1. Click ![Filter](https://files.readme.io/a5bfe04-Journey_Filter_Icon.png) beside the *Search* bar.

This displays the **Filter Journeys** box

2. Select the field to filter journeys by.
- The *Entry Type* enables you to filter by  
--  Live
-- Past Behavior
- The *Status* enables categories same as quick filters.
- The *Created by* enables you to filter by the journey creator using their email address. 
- The *Label* enables you to filter journeys by any specific labels that were applied to journeys.
3. Click **Apply**
This displays journeys with your applied filter.
4. Clicking **Save Filter** will add your applied filter to the quick filters on top.
Give your saved filter a name.
