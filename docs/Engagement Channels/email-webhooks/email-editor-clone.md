# Overview

You can use the Email Editor to add content to pre-built templates or customize and build your own templates. After creating an email template, you can use it in the current campaign or save it for future campaigns.

<Image title="Email-Editor-Overview.png" alt={1247} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/6ef5dac-Email-Editor-Overview.png" />

# Create Your First Email Template with the Email Editor

## Step 1: Navigate to the Email Editor

 Go to the Engage tab in the CleverTap dashboard, click on the Campaigns button, and then click on the + Campaign button.

<Image title="Campaign_main_list.png" alt={1436} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/957fd97-Campaign_main_list.png" />

Select the Email channel.

<Image title="Campaign_Email_Select_Channel.png" alt={1437} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/1553d7d-Campaign_Email_Select_Channel.png" />

## Step 2: Define the When

<Image title="Campaign_Email_Campaign_Type.png" alt={1440} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/0b65bee-Campaign_Email_Campaign_Type.png" />

Select the campaign type that you want. Then click Continue.

<Image title="Campaign_Email_Message_When.png" alt={1440} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/a82de8c-Campaign_Email_Message_When.png" />

## Step 3: Define the Who

<Image title="Campaign_Email_All_users.png" alt={1440} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/31f7368-Campaign_Email_All_users.png" />

Build your segment. Then click Continue.

<Image title="Campaigns_Adhoc_Segment.png" alt={949} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/c87c61f-Campaigns_Adhoc_Segment.png" />

# Define the What

This brings you to the Email Editor tool.

<Image title="Campaign_Email_What_Message.png" alt={1438} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/d77892d-Campaign_Email_What_Message.png" />

## Step 2: Choose a Template

Decide the template that you want and click Select.

You can choose between pre-built templates under Gallery or empty templates under Templates. You can also save customized templates and view them under My Gallery.

<Image title="Gallery-Step-2.png" alt={1243} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/7b817ba-Gallery-Step-2.png" />

Let's choose a pre-built template and explore a few ways to edit a template in Step 3.

## Step 3: Edit a Template

There are many edits you can make to a template. In this step, we will look at three of them: how to change an image, add a new block, and delete an element.

### Change an Image

1. Click inside the box of the image you want to change.

<Image title="Gallery-Step-3.png" alt={1236} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/c17df94-Gallery-Step-3.png" />

2. Click Change image.

<Image title="Gallery-Step-4.png" alt={1240} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/5c9f1bd-Gallery-Step-4.png" />

3. Drag the new image from your computer into the Empty Folder box.

<Image title="Gallery-Step-5.png" alt={1206} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/9aef8fd-Gallery-Step-5.png" />

4. Click Insert.

<Image title="Gallery-Step-6.png" alt={1189} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/117c025-Gallery-Step-6.png" />

Your new image will appear in the selected box.

<Image title="Gallery-Step-7.png" alt={1188} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/7912e68-Gallery-Step-7.png" />

### Add a New Text Block

1. Under the Content tab, find Text.

<Image title="Feature-Add-New-Text-Step-1.png" alt={1205} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/39c5f81-Feature-Add-New-Text-Step-1.png" />

2. Drag the Text icon to the desired location.

<Image title="Feature-Add-New-Text-Step-2.png" alt={1203} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/fff2fe4-Feature-Add-New-Text-Step-2.png" />

Now you have a new text block.

<Image title="Feature-Add-New-Text-Step-3.png" alt={1204} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/059a4ba-Feature-Add-New-Text-Step-3.png" />

### Delete an Element

1. Click inside the box of the element you want to delete.

<Image title="Feature-Delete-Element-Step-1.png" alt={1199} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/ddef033-Feature-Delete-Element-Step-1.png" />

2. Click the trashcan icon.

<Image title="Feature-Delete-Element-Step-2.png" alt={1203} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/8fc3166-Feature-Delete-Element-Step-2.png" />

3. Click Delete.

<Image title="Feature-Delete-Element-Step-3.png" alt={1200} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/12166d8-Feature-Delete-Element-Step-3.png" />

The element you deleted will no longer appear.

<Image title="Feature-Delete-Element-Step-4.png" alt={1205} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/6c1b6b0-Feature-Delete-Element-Step-4.png" />

## Step 4: Save A Template

1. Click Save to My Gallery.

<Image title="Save-Template-Step-1.png" alt={1203} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/85fee0f-Save-Template-Step-1.png" />

2. Name your template.

<Image title="Save-Template-Step-2.png" alt={1200} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/908b5b1-Save-Template-Step-2.png" />

3. Click Save.

<Image title="Save-Template-Step-3.png" alt={1200} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/a76ae05-Save-Template-Step-3.png" />

4. Click OK.

<Image title="Save-Template-Step-4.png" alt={1196} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/14acb53-Save-Template-Step-4.png" />

Your template will now be saved in My Gallery.

# FAQ

## How do I delete a saved template?

At this time, it is not possible to delete a template that has been saved.

## What if I want to choose a different template?

Click Change template at the top right.

<Image title="Email-Editor-FAQ1-1.png" alt={1254} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/bcf3f30-Email-Editor-FAQ1-1.png" />

Click OK.

<Image title="Email-Editor-FAQ1-2.png" alt={1240} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/4401d3a-Email-Editor-FAQ1-2.png" />

## I received the error message: "Please set the email credentials". How do I fix it?
