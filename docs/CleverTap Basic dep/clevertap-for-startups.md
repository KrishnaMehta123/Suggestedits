# Overview

CleverTap ignites your business growth by helping you retain users. It offers an all-encompassing automated, scalable, and secure ecosystem; CleverTap helps companies understand, segment, and engage users in real-time using context and powerful AI/ML models.  
Whether your users live inside your app or need a push to come back, our messaging and engagement tools cover you and help you deliver the ultimate omni-channel brand experience.

- **[App](https://docs.clevertap.com/docs/set-up-dashboard#set-up-app?utm_source=C4S&utm_medium=doc&utm_campaign=KB)**: The CleverTap SDK, when integrated with your app, can help in analytics and segmentation by collecting rich first-party data and activity trends across different devices and operating systems, so you can connect with users in the moments that matter most. CleverTap helps track previous app users, too, as long as they have an updated version of your app, which is integrated with the cleverTap SDK.
- **[Website](https://docs.clevertap.com/docs/set-up-dashboard#set-up-your-website?utm_source=C4S&utm_medium=doc&utm_campaign=KB)**: CleverTap can be integrated into a website to identify and track users. Although not all of the features of CleverTap can be used for web-based applications compared to mobile applications, features, including notifications, can be used to understand and engage with the users, thereby retaining and converting them.

Powered by [TesseractDBTM](https://clevertap.com/blog/tesseractdb-the-future-of-martech-is-here/utm_source=C4S&utm_medium=doc&utm_campaign=KB), the world's first purpose-built database, it helps you pursue user engagement and retention, delivering incredible performance, lightning-fast data processing, and unlimited, secure storage capacity.
