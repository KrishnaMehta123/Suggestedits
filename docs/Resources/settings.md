# Overview

Account Settings is where you can find and edit details of your account, manage events and user properties, and integrate campaigns.

1. Click the profile icon. This will display more profile options.

<Image title="Settings Step 1.png" alt={1266} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/0611aa5-Settings_Step_1.png" />

2. Click Settings.

<Image title="Settings Step 2.png" alt={1259} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/b9b9ca8-Settings_Step_2.png" />

You are now in Account Settings.

<Image title="Settings Step 3.png" alt={1264} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/7c5cede-Settings_Step_3.png" />

# How To Find Your Account Credentials

1. From the Account Settings page, locate your credentials in the top left box.

<Image title="Account Credentials Step 1.png" alt={1264} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/1ad2003-Account_Credentials_Step_1.png" />

2. Click the eye icon to reveal the value you are looking for.

<Image title="Account Credentials Step 2.png" alt={772} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/96e81e8-Account_Credentials_Step_2.png" />

## How To Invite A User

1. From the Account Settings page, locate your credentials in the top right box.

<Image title="Invite User Step 1.png" alt={1264} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/f0a9886-Invite_User_Step_1.png" />

2. Click the first box to enter the user's email address.

<Image title="Invite User Step 2.png" alt={370} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/37b46d3-Invite_User_Step_2.png" />

3. Click the second box to select the user's role. You can assign the user as Admin, Creator, Member or [Agent]().

<Image title="Invite User Step 3.png" alt={372} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/c15c47c-Invite_User_Step_3.png" />

4. Click the Invite button.

<Image title="Invite User Step 4.png" alt={370} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/20e4d0f-Invite_User_Step_4.png" />

# Features: Account

From the Account Settings page, scroll down to get to the Account section. Here you will see features related to your account.

<Image title="Features - Account Step 1.png" alt={1258} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/dcca4b8-Features_-_Account_Step_1.png" />

## Billing

On the Billing page, you can track app usage, upgrade your plan, or edit credit card details and billing contact. You can also view and export invoices.

<Image title="Billing.png" alt={1259} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/8b1e570-Billing.png" />

## Users

On the Users page, you can view users, edit their roles, and invite more users.

<Image title="Users.png" alt={1265} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/2aa046d-Users.png" />

## Profile

On the Profile page, you can view profile details and enable 2-step verification.

<Image title="Profile.png" alt={1262} className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/c0e33f7-Profile.png" />

## Account Timezone

On the Account Timezone page, you can change the timezone for your account.
