CleverTap Support Service Window (SSW) Explained

Service Window (SSW):\
Defined as 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM Local Standard Time, excluding holidays and weekends. All support communication is through CleverTap dashboard (a copy is sent to email IDâ€™s specified in support request).

Priorities Of Issues

P1 (High): 

* You are reporting an issue in the product that is impacting your end user engagement and there is no workaround available. Eg Campaign went out to wrong users or campaign not going out atall. Our support team shall acknowledge your issue in 2 working hours

P2 (Medium):\
-You are reporting an issue with one of the product features and there is a workaround available. Our support team shall acknowledge your issue in 4 working hours. E.g. Funnel number mismatch

P3 (Low)

* You are raising a query on how a feature works to need additional information on the product. Our support team shall acknowledge your issue in 4 working hours. Eg. Unable to sort a table

Priority review and re-priority\
Our support team may downgrade the severity level if it is not in accordance with the impact stated above or if you are not able to provide adequate resources or responses to enable us to continue with problem resolution efforts. The frequency of status update may be redefined with mutual agreement for a support request.

Exceptions:\
We do provide 24/7 support for high priority bugs that is identified as a showstopper for our business
